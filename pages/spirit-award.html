Criteria for Award
Criteria for the award include outstanding scholarship, leadership, campus involvement, community service, and communication skills. A committee of faculty and staff interviews the students who are advanced to the final round of the application process. The Spirit of Maryland Award is presented at the halftime of the Homecoming football game.

To be eligible for the Spirit of Maryland Award, a student must:

  • Be recognized by the University as being of senior status
  • Have 90+ academic credits*
  • Have a cumulative grade point average of 3.0 or above.
  • If the student is expected to graduate by May of the following semester, and does not have the current 90 academic credit senior status, (s)he may still be eligible to apply.

Questions? Email Joe Calizo at jcalizo@umd.edu.
