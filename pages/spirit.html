Homecoming Spirit Days
Homecoming is about celebrating the University of Maryland and we want to make sure that everyone has their chance to wear their best gold, black, white, and red outfits. If you're on campus please join us in celebrating every color of our alma mater.

  • Monday, October 23: RED
  • Tuesday, October 24: WHITE
  • Wednesday, October 25: GOLD
  • Thursday, October 26: BLACK
  • Friday, October 27: ALL MARYLAND COLORS

Homecoming Office Decorating Competition
With Homecoming approaching quickly, show your Terrapin Pride by decking out your office suites! It’s our Third Annual Homecoming Office Decorating Competition so we encourage you to “Beat the Hoosiers!” Your participation will get students, faculty, and staff in the Homecoming Spirit as well as give your office a chance to be our Grand Prize Winner! The competition is open to all. So come one, come all!

"Fearless Ideas" Ice Cream
This limited-edition Maryland Dairy ice cream debuting on Oct. 27 reflects the university’s global influence. It starts with Maryland cream blended with Free Trade Latin American and European chocolates and a hint of Madagascar vanilla. This chocolate base is then swirled with sweetened Italian mascarpone cheese and melted chocolate truffles blended with a splash of Sagamore Spirits Maryland rye whiskey. It will be available only at the Stamp and select concession stands at Maryland Stadium.
