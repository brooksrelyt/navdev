Parking

Visitor Parking
Visitor Parking is available in 4 parking garages and several surface lots. Most visitors use the Digital Pay Stations to park, which accept cash or credit.

Parking Map (PDF) →

Game Attendee Parking
All attendees are encouraged to purchase game day parking permits in advance. Parking permits may be purchased on-site at Terrapin Trail Garage if space is available. The cost of a single game permit is $17.

Current students and employees should browse the Parking on Game Day section of this website to learn how game days will impact travel to campus.

Terp Carnival Parking
Guests planning on attending the Terp Carnival after 4 p.m. on Friday, October 27 are encouraged to park in lots C1 and C2, or Lot 1.
